Campus Life

📅 2024-04-09 📂 campus

I was assigned to live in NUS UTown Residence, which located at the very nice position in the NUS. Sometimes you can also see some tourist near the residence. UTR was planned to become the Olympics residence; however, because during construction, the high expense caused multiple delay,. As the result, other place became Olympics residence instead.

My room type is 4-person family room, which is quite comfy. I lived here during Jan. to May. For me, I start to turn on air-conditioner from Mid-March. 12~2 is the cool season in Singapore. It’s comfortable with fan.

I used to go out for meals, though there has some restaurants near UTR, I still prefer go to Clementi for meals. It takes about 25mins to get there by walking. For meals I will write different chapter to introduce some Singapore cuisines.
